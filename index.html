A bit about myself.

ARMA SERIES: I’m a big fan of the Arma series and DayZ modding scene and have always enjoyed creating things just for the fun of it. I’m really into anything military related, and Arma has always felt like the perfect place to bring ideas to life. I started learning the Enfusion Engine back in October 2025 and have mainly been focused on map design, with more recent work getting into modelling as well. I’m still learning as I go, but I enjoy figuring things out and pushing myself to learn more. I’ve always been a bit of a nerd when it comes to computers and tech, and I’m naturally curious about how everything works behind the scenes.

DAYZ: I’m also the owner of an Xbox DayZ community server with almost 1,000 members that I built completely from scratch. Over the last 2 years of running the server, I’ve learned a lot about the development side of DayZ, including working with JSON and XML, alongside a large amount of custom map development work. Everything I’ve made has been handcrafted because I’ve always enjoyed the journey and learning process more than the destination itself. Building and running a community from the ground up has taught me a lot, and honestly I’ve loved every moment of it.
